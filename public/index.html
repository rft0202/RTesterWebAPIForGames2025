Things I Like: Food Edition





Why I Chose This Topic

The topic options given were food, video games, movies, or places I have visited.
I couldn't think of a lot of movies, and I haven't traveled many places.
I feel that if I share my favorite video games, I won't beat the "not a gamer" allegations.
I felt listing my favorite foods was the safest option. It might be obvious, but I have a sweet tooth.
Sweets aren't the only thing I like, though. New York Pizza is the best, no contest.
